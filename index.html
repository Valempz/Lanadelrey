ÁLBUMES DE LANA DEL REY

Born to Die is the second studio album and debut major-label record by Lana Del Rey. It was released on January 27, 2012, by Interscope Records, Polydor Records, and Stranger Records.
Ultraviolence is the third studio album by singer Lana Del Rey. It was released on June 13, 2014 by UMG Recordings.
Honeymoon is the fourth studio album and third major-label record by Lana Del Rey. It was released on September 18, 2015, by UMG Recordings.
Lust for Life is the fifth studio album and fourth studio album on a major label by Lana Del Rey. It is expected to be released on July 21, 2017, by Polydor and Interscope Records.
Norman Fucking Rockwell! It is the sixth studio album by American singer Lana Del Rey; released on August 30, 2019 by Polydor and Interscope Records
Chemtrails over the Country Club —en español: Estelas químicas sobre el club de campo— es el séptimo álbum de estudio de la cantautora estadounidense Lana Del Rey. Fue lanzado el 19 de marzo de 2021 por Interscope Records y Polydor Records como continuación de su sexto álbum de estudio, Norman Fucking Rockwell! (2019).
Blue Banisters —in Spanish: Barandillas Azules— is the eighth studio album by American singer-songwriter Lana Del Rey. It was released on October 22, 2021 by Interscope Records and Polydor Records.
Did You Know That There's a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd (stylized in sentence case) is the upcoming ninth studio album by American singer-songwriter Lana Del Rey. It is scheduled for release on March 24, 2023, by Interscope and Polydor Records.
